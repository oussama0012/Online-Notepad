Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of Online Notepad (https://onlinenotepad.space/). This document outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our online notepad service.

By using Online Notepad, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

Online Notepad is designed to respect your privacy. We prioritize your data security in the following ways:

  • Text Data: Any text you enter in the notepad is temporarily stored in your browser's local storage. This data never reaches our servers and remains exclusively on your device.
  • No Account Required: We do not require user registration or accounts, eliminating the need to collect personal information.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage information to improve our service, such as browser type, features used, and time spent on the site.

Local Storage

Online Notepad uses your browser's local storage to save your notes temporarily. This means:

  • Your data is stored locally on your device, not on our servers
  • Your notes persist between sessions when using the same browser and device
  • Clearing your browser data will remove saved notes
  • We cannot access or recover your notes if they are deleted

Cookies

Our service may use "cookies" to enhance your experience. These are small files placed on your device to collect standard internet log information and visitor behavior information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Analytics

This data is shared with other Google services. Google may use the collected data to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network. For more information on the privacy practices of Google, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms web page: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Security
